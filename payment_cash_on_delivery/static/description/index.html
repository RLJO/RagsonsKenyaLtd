Payment is one of the most crucial parts of a business for the flawless management. The main motive of every business is to deliver customer satisfaction. When making the purchase online many customers are afraid to pay before the delivery. Hence, most of the people prefer to pay for their order after delivery.

Cash on Delivery facilitates your customers to check their order before making the payment. Hence, it builds a strong relationship with the customers; as it makes your websites credible for your customers.

Now, manage cash on delivery in Odoo without any hassle. Odoo Website COD Payment Acquirer module adds Cash on Delivery as the mode of Payment on the Odoo Website. The customers can now pay via cash for the orders. The admin can set rules for availing COD facility on the Odoo website. Each transaction is noted in the Odoo with the reference to the Sales order.

Offer Flexible Payment Option to the customers in your Odoo Website

One of the most important benefits of cash on delivery is that customers can pay only after receiving the product in hand. Hence, it avoids the risk of money loss.

The customer is free to pay as per his/her convenience as there is no dependency on cards or other online payment modes.

The more ease the customers will find in shopping with you, the more it will increase their chances to shop with you.

Set the Criteria for Cash on delivery in Odoo as per your requirement

The flexibility offered to your customers should also have some set of criteria so that you don’t need to face complications and continue the flawless service without any hassle.

Coming to Cash on Delivery in Odoo, now you can set the minimum or maximum shopping cart value required to avail the COD option for any order.

Moreover, you can also restrict the cash on delivery payment option for selected countries, states, or zip codes.

Detailed Features List

Offer Cash On Delivery Payment Option
  • The module adds COD as a payment method on Odoo website.
  • As an admin, you can configure the COD payment method from the backend.
Payment Flexibility offered to the customers
  • Allow your customers to make payments at the time of delivery.
  • Buyer can check the COD availability at the product page.
Set the criteria to Avail the COD Payment Option
  • You can set the minimum and maximum shopping cart value for customers to avail cash on delivery options.
  • Also, you can exclude specific Products From COD.
Restrict Cash on Delivery For Particular Area
  • You can restrict the COD payment method to selected countries, states or even zip codes.
Set the custom message to show your customers
  • You can configure custom messages for COD availability and unavailability
  • You can also set COD policies to be visible on the product page.
Appearance of Custom Message in Odoo Website
  • A custom message appears while selecting COD for an order consisting of products without COD availability.

Enabled COD Options

After the Installation of this module you will able see the COD payment Acquirer method in Side Setting->Payment->Payment Acquirers .

Here You can Enable/Disabled it's Website Visibility and also select/Create Custom COD Availability Rule.

Inside COD Availability Rule Admin can:

  1. Set Minimum Order Amount to use the COD.
  2. Set Maximum Order Amount to use the COD.
  3. Select Excluded Product from COD.
  4. Enabled/Disable Expected Date of Delivery on Website Product Page.
  5. Enabled/Disable COD Policy of on Website Product Page.
  6. Set Custom Message Content for COD Policy.
  7. Set Custom Message Content to show on Availability of product for COD.
  8. Set Custom Message Content to show on Unavailability of product for COD.


Module Also Provide Country based COD will applicability:

  1. Admin Can Enable Country-wise COD applicability.
  2. Or Enable Country-wise and State-wise COD applicability.
  3. Or Enable Country-wise and ZipCode-wise COD applicability.
  4. Or Even Enable Country-wise, State-wise and ZipCode-wise COD applicability.
  5. At a time Admin Can Enable Multiple Country For COD Applicability.

At the checkout page, Select the Mangopay as a payment option, now click on “pay now” button.

COD Availability

During the website visit the user will see the COD Availability Information on the Product View.

Note:
The Expected Date of Delivery Represent the Sum of Current Date and sale_delay set for product.

COD Payment

On the payment page the COD payment option will available and show You have Selected [Cash On Delivery] Payment Option this Sale Order to users message on selection COD Payment Options.
As if the cart contains all those product for which COD is set to Availability by Odoo Admin, then user can Choose COD Payment method.

In case the Cart contain the COD Unavailable product then COD payment will set to disable and display the custom message about COD Unavailability set by Admin for Odoo Back-End on the hover of COD Payment Option
